# General
Frequently Asked Questions

## Why isn't Watchtower on the Google Play Store?
**Watchtower** won't be on the **Google Play Store**.

APK-based extensions conflict with [Google Play's content policy](https://play.google.com/about/developer-content-policy/).
**Google** might take down the app due to certain content, which the developers wishes to avoid.

## Is Watchtower available for iOS/iPadOS?
There is no iOS or iPadOS version and neither are there plans for one.
Porting is difficult due to the separate codebases of iOS and Android apps.

Any app proclaiming to be "**Watchtower for iOS**" is not by us and should be treated as a scam.

## What is Watchtower Preview?
**Watchtower Preview** is a regularly updated beta version of the app.
It showcases potential upcoming features, but it's more prone to bugs and crashes.

::: warning CAUTION
While it's ideal for users seeking the latest **Watchtower** experience, it's essential to keep [auto-backup](/docs/guides/backups#enabling-automatic-backups) enabled to prevent library loss due to issues.
:::

## Can I read light novels?
Yes! **Watchtower** includes a dedicated **light novel (LN) text reader** that supports LN extensions and local `.epub`/`.txt` files.

See the [Light Novel reader guide](/docs/guides/light-novel-reader) for full instructions on getting started with LN sources, custom fonts, and reading settings.

## What content types does Watchtower support?
**Watchtower** supports three main content types:
- **Watch** — stream from online sources or play local video files.
- **Manga** — read from online sources or local image files/archives.
- **Light Novels** — read from LN extension sources or local `.epub`/`.txt` files.

## What's a fork?
Forks are alternate **Watchtower** versions with distinct features.
Get more details [here](/forks/).
